Key Principles

1

Deny Trauma

Your past does not determine your present. What matters is not what happened to you, but how you interpret and use those experiences.

2

Embrace Discomfort

The courage to be happy includes the courage to be disliked. You cannot live freely if you are constantly seeking approval from others.

3

Focus on Contribution

True happiness comes from contributing to others and to the common good, not from competing or comparing yourself to others.

4

Choose Your Response

You always have the freedom to choose how you respond to any situation. This choice is the foundation of your happiness and freedom.
